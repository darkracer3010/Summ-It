About Us

Welcome to our video summarization website! Our platform is designed to help you quickly and easily summarize any video content you come across Youtube. Whether you're a busy professional who needs to quickly understand the key takeaways from a long video, or a student trying to study more efficiently, our platform has you covered.

Our platform is incredibly user-friendly. Simply enter the link to the video you want to summarize, and our algorithm will get to work analyzing the content.Within minutes, you'll receive a summary of the video in the form of a text.

We first encountered this issue in college when we would watch very long YouTube videos while looking for material relating to academics but were left with irrelevant information. We then had the idea to build a website that allows users to view a summary of a video before deciding whether or not to watch it.

Thank you for choosing our website for your video summarization needs. We look forward to helping you get the most out of your video content!
